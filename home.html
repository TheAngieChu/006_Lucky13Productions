So, you've finished a novel!! You hit PUBLISH and wait on the Big Bucks to come rolling in.

But a month passes by. Then, two. Three. No one is buying your book. You've resulted to harassing friends and family to read it.

Ah, the classic tale of a hidden gem in the vast literary ocean. Your book, a masterpiece, yet it's like whispering a secret in a bustling marketplace; it's there, but who's really hearing it? Here's the twist in the plot: most authors, incredibly talented in weaving stories, often find themselves at a crossroads when it comes to marketing their creation. That's where the magic gets a tad lost, not in your story, but in making it visible to the world.


Enter the scene: Lucky 13 Productions. Think of us as that quirky side character in your story who knows all the secret pathways and hidden alleys. We are here not just to tell you that marketing is important – that's the obvious part – but to guide you through the enchanting (yet sometimes baffling) world of book marketing. With our treasure trove of solid advice and engaging training videos, we turn the 'how' and 'what' of marketing into a journey worth embarking on.


Embarking on a writing career is akin to setting sail on a grand adventure. And every adventurer needs a compass and a map. Lucky 13 Productions is here to equip you with these essential tools. Our expertise is your guiding star, leading you to the shores of success. We don't just help you shout into the void; we teach you how to echo in the halls of your soon-to-be fans.
